# before staring
1. Connect a headphone to the Arduino (only aux)
How to do that ?
      Connect to two pins in the top with a resistor to the "D9" Pin of you Arduino
      After doing that connect the buttom pin(s) to the "GND" Pin of your Arduino
2. Upload the sketch
